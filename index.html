Here's a timeline of Dead By Daylight's life cycle:

  • June 14th, 2016 Dead by Daylight is released.
  • August 18th, 2016 Chapter 1: The Last Breath is released.
  • October 25th, 2016 The HALLOWEEN® Chapter is released. This is when the game really began gaining traction.
  • December 8th, 2016 Chapter 3: Of Flesh and Mud is released.
  • May 11th, 2017 Chapter 4: Spark of Madness is released.
  • July 27th, 2017 Chapter 5: A Lullaby for the Dark is released.
  • October 26th, 2017 Chapter 6: A Nightmare on Elm Street™ is released.
